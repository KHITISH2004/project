Ingredients

  • 500g mutton pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan and saut√© the onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the mutton pieces and brown them on all sides.
  4. Add turmeric, red chili powder, cumin powder, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add the tomato puree and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add yogurt and mix well. Cover and cook the mutton on low heat for 45 minutes or until tender.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander.
  8. Serve hot with rice or roti.
