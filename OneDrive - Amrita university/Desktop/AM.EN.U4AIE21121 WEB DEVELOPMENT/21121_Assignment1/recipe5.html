Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Coat chicken with olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill for 6-8 minutes on each side.
