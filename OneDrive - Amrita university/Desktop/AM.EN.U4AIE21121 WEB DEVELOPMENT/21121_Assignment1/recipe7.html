Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables until tender.
  3. Add soy sauce and cook for another 2 minutes.
