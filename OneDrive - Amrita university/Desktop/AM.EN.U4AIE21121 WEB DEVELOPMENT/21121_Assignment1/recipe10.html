Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Melt the butter and mix with sugar.
  3. Add eggs, cocoa powder, and flour.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
