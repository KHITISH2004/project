Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Mash the avocados in a bowl.
  2. Add lime juice, onion, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
  3. Mix until well combined.
