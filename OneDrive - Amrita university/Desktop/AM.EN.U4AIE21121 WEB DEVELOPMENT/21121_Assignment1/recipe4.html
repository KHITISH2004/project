Ingredients

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • Croutons
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop the lettuce.
  2. Toss with Caesar dressing.
  3. Top with Parmesan, croutons, and pepper.
