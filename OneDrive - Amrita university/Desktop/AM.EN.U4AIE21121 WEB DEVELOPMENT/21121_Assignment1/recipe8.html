Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets (e.g., tilapia, salmon)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add turmeric, red chili powder, cumin, and coriander powder. Stir well.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Gently add the fish fillets and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning carefully.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or naan.
