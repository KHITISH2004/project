Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Whisk in milk and egg until smooth.
  3. Cook on a greased skillet until bubbles form, then flip.
