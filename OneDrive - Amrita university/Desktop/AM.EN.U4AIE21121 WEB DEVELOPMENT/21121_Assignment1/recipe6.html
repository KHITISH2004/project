Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, and cinnamon.
  2. Dip bread slices in mixture.
  3. Cook on a greased skillet until golden brown on each side.
